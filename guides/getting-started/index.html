First Steps

When you log in for the first time you'll find yourself in Lumbridge. Tutorial Island walks you through the absolute basics — combat, skilling, the interface. Complete it fully; the XP rewards are meaningful at low levels.

A few principles that anchor everything else:

  • Quest early. Quests grant large XP lamps, unlock shortcuts, and gate the best content. Many efficient routes front-load quests precisely because of this.
  • Bank often. Inventory management is a genuine mechanic. Knowing what to keep, drop, or bank at each stage matters.
  • Read tooltips. Every skill, item, and NPC has contextual information in-game. The game rewards curiosity.

Phase 1 — Early Game

Roughly levels 1–40 across combat skills. The goal here is unlocking the baseline content that makes everything else accessible.

Combat foundation

Train Attack, Strength, and Defence in parallel or in sequence depending on your preferred style. Melee is the most resource-efficient combat style for early levels — no ammunition or rune costs.
